- Expert appraiser testimony generally required  
- Appeals go to Court of Appeals, not Supreme Court  
- Confirmation failure bars deficiency action only, not pursuit of other collateral  
- Multiple properties securing one debt require separate confirmations  
- Guarantor waiver provisions must be explicitly drafted
